Maston’s Food Program

Week 1:

Your Task:

  • Water
    • Continue to earn your smiles for the day for hydration.
  • Oatmeal
    • Eat cooked oatmeal 2x this week for breakfast.
Bonus Points: Incorporating an additional apple, orange or pear in the afternoon on oatmeal days giving you an extra “Fruit Point”. If you are eating a banana with your oatmeal that also counts towards the fruit point goal.
Goal:
2 Oatmeal Points
Stretch Goal:
4 Fruit Points (note: Your Oatmeal fruit rolls into this number.)

Week 2:

Your Task:

  • Water
    • Continue to earn your smiles for the day for hydration.
  • Oatmeal
    • Eat cooked oatmeal 3x this week for breakfast.
  • Fruit
    • Every Oatmeal day you eat a piece of fruit.
Bonus Points: Incorporating an additional apple, orange or pear in the afternoon on oatmeal days.
Goal:
3 Oatmeal Points
Stretch Goal:
6 Fruit Points (note: Your Oatmeal fruit rolls into this number.)
(italic) It is important to note that it is after Week 2 that participants have started to see the fiber “doing its job.” As Tank says to Neo, “This is an exciting time.”

Week 3:

Your Task:

  • Water
    • Continue to earn your smiles for the day for hydration.
  • Oatmeal
    • Eat cooked oatmeal 4x this week for breakfast. (or move to every day)
  • Fruit
    • One piece of fruit a day.
Bonus Points: Incorporating raw veggies once a day. Cut carrots, broccoli or whatever you like to crunch on. Do not add ranch dressing.
Goal:
4 Oatmeal Points
5 Fruit Points (note: Your Oatmeal fruit rolls into this number.)
Stretch Goal:
9 Fruit Points (note: Your Oatmeal fruit rolls into this goal.)
5 Veggie Points

Week 4:

Your Task:

  • Water
    • Continue to earn your smiles for the day for hydration.
  • Oatmeal
    • Eat cooked oatmeal 5x this week for breakfast. (or move to every day)
  • Fruit
    • One piece of fruit a day.
  • Veggies
    • One raw veggie a day. Do not add ranch dressing.

Goal:
5 Oatmeal Points
5 Fruit Points (note: Your Oatmeal fruit rolls into this number.)
5 Veggie Points (I’m serious about the no dressing thing. -1 for dressing.)
Stretch Goal:
Start to investigate other ways to get more fiber in your daily routine. Visit the hacks page for more info.

Week 5 thru Week 8

You will repeat what you did on Week 4 for the next four weeks. Now starting to explore new options for your other meals.

That’s completely up to you.
I have friends that started to make chicken tenders with wasa crackers and even their kids loved them. Baked Chick Peas as a great snack to have around that is “fiberlicious” (a term coined by Candice DiCocco).
